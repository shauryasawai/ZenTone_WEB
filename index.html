Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is ZenTone's emotion detection?

▼

ZenTone achieves 98.13% accuracy in facial expression analysis and 87% accuracy in voice tone analysis using advanced AI models trained on comprehensive datasets.

Do I need special hardware to use ZenTone?

▼

No! ZenTone works with your existing devices - smartphones, tablets, and computers. Optional integrations with wearables and EEG devices are available for enhanced functionality.

Is my data secure and private?

▼

Absolutely. ZenTone is HIPAA compliant with AES-256 encryption, on-device processing when possible, and strict data minimization principles. Your privacy is our priority.

Can healthcare providers use ZenTone?

Yes! ZenTone Clinical offers provider dashboards, EHR integration, patient management tools, and clinical-grade analytics designed specifically for healthcare professionals.
